# Retail Market Analysis Dashboard (2010–2013)
This dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of retail sales, profit, discounts, shipping costs, customer segments, and regional performance over the last four years. It helps identify sales trends, profit growth, customer behavior, and business performance across different product categories and regions.
## Tools Used
 * Excel Dashboard
 * Pivot Tables
 * Pivot Charts
 * Slicers
 * Data Visualization
## Key Insights
1. Total Sales: $8.95M+ generated during the analysis period.
2. Total Profit: $692K+ profit achieved across all regions.
3. Profit Growth: Profit increased consistently from 2010 to 2013.
4. Regional Performance: Central region generated the highest sales contribution (28%).
5. Category Discount: Office Supplies received the highest discount share (55.58%).
6. Top Sub-Categories: Telephones & Communication generated the highest profit.
7. Daily Sales Trend: Sales remained strong throughout the week, with peak sales on Monday.
